Record and save your workout for the day.

We've created a place to record your workouts from the weight you lifted down to the sets/repetitions you've complated. Lets make notebooks a thing a past by having your workouts saved safely online. Look back at old workouts and see the progress you've made.

How did this site come to be?

For the longest time I, Jose Munoz, struggled to find an app or page where I could record my numbers for the day at the gym. I didn't want to carry a notebook around because I felt this was annoying or I'd be worried something would happen to the notebook. Perhaps, the biggest issue would be that I would forget it at home. I figured there would be an app out there that resolve this issue but I didn't find one or they were hidden behind fees I personally didn't want to pay. So I started developing this page and possible future app to fill that need. I hope after it is all said and done that this will be something to help me but also help other reach goals and see their progress on their fitness journey.
